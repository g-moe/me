Static sites are still a great default for personal websites and blogs.

They are simple to host, quick to load, and easy to reason about. For a personal
blog, that matters more than having a complex publishing system.

## Benefits

- The pages are built ahead of time.
- Hosting can stay cheap and boring.
- There is no database to keep alive.
- Content lives next to the code.

Cloudflare Pages fits this model well because it can build the site and serve the
result globally without much ceremony.

That is enough for a normal blog.
