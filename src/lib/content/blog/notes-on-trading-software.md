Trading software has a simple promise: show the right information at the right time.

That sounds obvious, but it shapes a lot of product decisions. A chart, scanner, or
execution tool should reduce hesitation instead of adding another layer to decode.

## What matters most

1. Clear state
2. Fast feedback
3. Predictable controls
4. Easy recovery from mistakes

When those pieces are handled well, the tool starts to feel quiet. It gets out of
the way and lets the trader focus on the decision.

[Tradester](https://tradester.com) is one place where I think about these tradeoffs
often.
